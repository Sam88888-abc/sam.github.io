Admissions

At Infinity Knowledge University, we welcome students from all backgrounds to begin their academic journey with us. Whether you are a local or international applicant, our admissions process is clear, guided, and supportive every step of the way.


Programs Available:

  • Diploma in Computer Science
  • Diploma in Business
  • Foundation in Business
  • Bachelor of Business
  • Bachelor of Computer Science

Admission Guide

Who Can Apply?

  • High school graduates with relevant qualifications
  • Diploma holders seeking credit transfer
  • International students with equivalent academic credentials

How to Apply?

  1. Send necessary documents (certificates, transcripts, IC) and tell us the program you want to resgister through our email IKUniversity@gmail.com
  2. Attend an interview (if required).
  3. Wait for offer letter via email.
  4. Confirm and proceed with enrollment & fee payment.
  5. Attend orientation and begin your academic journey!

Entry Requirements:

Undergraduate Programs: Minimum 5 credits in SPM / O-Level or equivalent.

Diploma Programs: Minimum 3 credits in SPM / O-Level or equivalent.


Tuition Fees:

  • Foundation Programs: MYR 5,000 per semester
  • Diploma Programs: MYR 6,500 per semester
  • Degree Programs: MYR 8,000 per semester
  • Postgraduate: Varies by course

Scholarships Available:

  • Merit-based Scholarships (SPM/UEC/STPM excellence)
  • Need-based Financial Aid
  • Athletic or Talent Scholarships
  • International Student Grants
