About Infinity Knowledge University

University Introduction

Infinity Knowledge University (IKU) is a forward-thinking institution committed to excellence in education, innovation, and research. Founded with a mission to empower future leaders, IKU offers a wide range of academic programmes across various disciplines, preparing students to succeed in a globalized world. With modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a strong emphasis on student development, IKU stands as a beacon of academic inspiration.


Our History

Established in 2005, Infinity Knowledge University began as a small college dedicated to technology and business studies. Over the years, it has expanded into a comprehensive university with diverse faculties and thousands of students from all over the region. IKU's history is marked by its commitment to continuous growth, innovation in teaching, and community engagement.
